Hey Fork! This is Abhishek, and in this web app you will find a classification model.

This model is build over ResNet50V2 architecture. Used to classify different environments

Data-set

Data-set used for the model is from Kaggle, you could find like here: Dataset Link

This data-set contains around 25k images i total under 6 different categories, named:

  • Buildings
  • Forest
  • Glacier
  • Mountain
  • Sea
  • Street
